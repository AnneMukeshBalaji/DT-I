In the evolving landscape of retail, ensuring a seamless and efficient checkout process is critical for enhancing customer experience and operational efficiency. Traditional checkout systems often involve long queues, human errors, and manual verification, which can negatively impact both customers and retailers. To address these challenges, this project introduces ScanPay, an innovative solution that leverages machine learning (ML) and computer vision techniques to automate and verify the barcode scanning and payment completion processes in real-time at the billing counter.

The system utilizes advanced object detection models, such as YOLO (You Only Look Once), to monitor and track customer actions, ensuring that each item is successfully scanned before proceeding with the payment. Additionally, the project integrates payment confirmation detection by recognizing visual feedback from the point-of-sale (POS) system or through customer behavior, such as gesture recognition, indicating a successful transaction. Real-time alerts are triggered if a product is left unscanned or the payment process is incomplete, prompting immediate corrective actions.

The implementation of this system brings several benefits, including the elimination of checkout lines, reduced human error in scanning and payment validation, and increased operational efficiency. Moreover, it enhances the customer experience by making the entire process faster and more convenient, providing a touchless, cashier-less shopping experience. By integrating real-time video processing, object detection, and machine learning models, ScanPay represents a significant step toward a smarter, more efficient retail environment.

This project showcases the potential of machine learning in revolutionizing retail checkout systems, offering a future-ready solution that can scale and adapt to various retail environments, providing both businesses and consumers with a more reliable and frictionless shopping experience
